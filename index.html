What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds and build wealth through disciplined investing.

How SIP Works

  • You invest a fixed amount every month
  • Money is invested in mutual funds
  • Returns grow due to compounding
  • Long-term investing reduces risk

Benefits of SIP

  • Disciplined savings habit
  • Affordable investment option
  • Power of compounding
  • Lower market timing risk

FAQs

Is SIP safe?
Yes, for long-term investment in good funds.

Can I stop SIP anytime?
Yes, SIP is flexible.

Minimum SIP amount?
Usually starts from ₹500.

SIP vs Lump sum?
SIP reduces market risk.
